Our Vision

To live a life that pleases the Lord and, through this, reflects Christ in our daily actions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make disciples of Christ by sharing the Gospel and living its teachings. We are committed to guiding others in their faith journey, helping them grow in their relationship with Jesus, and reflecting His love in our community. Through preaching, teaching, and leading by example, we aim to inspire and equip individuals to follow Christ and spread His message of hope and salvation.

About us

Welcome to Berean Global Methodist Church - Naivasha, where we are guided by the vision of Romans 12:1-2. We are called to present ourselves as living sacrifices, holy and pleasing to God, and to experience the renewal of our minds. Embracing this divine invitation, our church is dedicated to living a life of worship and transformation. Through vibrant worship, deep biblical teaching, and compassionate service, we strive to embody the change that Christ brings. Just as Paul encourages us to be transformed by the scripture, we believe that His grace can renew every part of our lives. Join us as we embark on this sacred journey, allowing God's transformative power to uplift and guide us, as we reflect His love and light in our community.
